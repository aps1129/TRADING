## 🧭 Navigating the App

The application is split into three main "Power Centers", accessible via the top navigation bar:

1. **Overview**: Your personalized market dashboard.
2. **News AI**: An automatic news intelligence engine.
3. **Predictions**: Your historical and predictive AI tracking room.

---

## 📊 1. The Overview (Dashboard)

This is your mission control. It gives you a bird's-eye view of everything happening in the markets currently.

### 📌 How to Track a Ticker (Adding Stocks to Your Watchlist)
Want to keep an eye on a specific company?
1. Click the glowing **+ TICKER** button.
2. Type in the **Symbol** (e.g., `RELIANCE` for Reliance Industries, `TSLA` for Tesla, `NIFTYBEES` for your favorite ETF).
3. (Optional) Give it a customized name.
4. Click **Submit**.
*Boom! The stock is now permanently pinned to your live Watchlist, streaming real-time prices and 24-hour change percentages.*

### 🔍 Global Search (The `CTRL+K` Bar)
At the top of your screen, there is a **Search Bar**.
* **Global Power:** Start typing any company name or ticker symbol. The app instantly connects to Yahoo Finance and searches the entire globe for your stock, ETF, or mutual fund.
* Once you see your asset drop down, simply click it to enter the **Trading Terminal**!

---

## 🔬 2. The Trading Terminal (Stock Details)

When you click on any stock in your watchlist or from the search bar, you'll be transported to the Terminal. 

### 🕯️ Cinematic Candlestick Charts
- The large chart in the center is plotting real-time **OHLC** (Open, High, Low, Close) pricing. Green means the price went up, Red means it went down. 
- **Timeframes**: Above the chart, you can click `1D`, `5D`, `1M`, or `1Y` to zoom in and out of the asset's history.
- **Intraday Mode**: Click the `Intraday (5m)` button to watch the live, minute-by-minute heartbeat of the stock.

### 🤖 AI Market Predictions
On the right side of the terminal, you'll see a dark glass panel named **AI Consensus**. 
* The moment you open the terminal, our **Llama 3.3 70B NLP Engine** reads the stock's volume, price history, technical indicators, and news—and spits out a human-readable summary of what it expects the stock to do next. Is it Bullish? Bearish? It will tell you with a confidence score!

### 🎯 Highlight Markers 
See those little colored arrows sitting on the candlestick chart? 
Our backend math engine actively scours the chart for famous Wall Street candlestick patterns (like `Doji`, `Hammer`, or `Bearish Engulfing`). 
* If you click on one of the pattern names in the list below the chart, the camera will smoothly **pan and zoom** directly to that exact spot on the chart so you can see the anomaly for yourself!

---

## 📰 3. News AI (Global Market Intel)

News moves the market, but reading 50 articles a day is impossible. Let the AI do it for you.

### ⚡ Force Sync DB
Click the **"FORCE SYNC DB"** button. 
* The application will immediately contact 6 top-tier financial news networks (like *Reuters*, *LiveMint*, *NDTV*), download the latest articles, and feed them directly into the **Gemini 2.0 AI Engine**.
* Gemini reads the articles, extracts whether the news is good (**Bullish**) or bad (**Bearish**) for the economy, highlights the most important 3 bullet points, and tags any specific companies mentioned.

### 🎛️ Dynamic Filtering
Once the news loads, you can click the neon colored filter tags at the top (`BULLISH`, `BEARISH`, `NEUTRAL`) to isolate the exact vibe of the market you want to read about.
